Key Achievements & Innovations

AI-Driven Process Automation at Curve

I developed a pioneering strategy at Curve to leverage AI for both design and localization, dramatically increasing efficiency and scalability. This flowchart outlines the human-in-the-loop system I created.

1. Initial Prompt & Asset Input

Creative briefs and design assets are fed into the system.

↓

2. AI Agent Processing

AI agents generate initial designs or translations based on predefined models and rules.

↓

3. Human Oversight & Review

Internal teams and 3rd-party agencies review, annotate, and provide feedback on AI output.

↓

4. Iterative Refinement (Round Trip)

Feedback is looped back to the AI agents for refinement, creating a continuous improvement cycle.

↓

5. Final Output & Integration

Approved assets are automatically integrated into tech stacks and release cycles.
